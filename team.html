We are three students of Licei Valdagno and we take scientific classes in the fourth year. We have different passions: maths, physics, english, astronomy and tecnology. Mixed all together they build space exploration. We build The Tiger Team! The name of our team takes inspiration from the Tiger Team created during Apollo 13 to fix several problems of the spacecraft. We, as well, in our project fix several problems that might affect the next International Space Station. Step by step, from september 2016 to march 2017 with weekly afternoon meetings, we analysed everything and we found solutions explained in our work.

The ODYSSEUS II Project has received funding from the European Union's Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme. It is funded with nearly two million Euros by the European Commission in the framework of EU HORIZON 2020, started on 1st January 2015 and will run until the 31st December 2017. A strong consortium of fourteen partners and four supporting organizations from eleven European countries will collaborate to provide learning opportunities in space science and technology to European youth. Italian partners are Politecnico di Torino and Altec.
