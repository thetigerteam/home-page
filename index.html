This is the website created by the team to host our work in a more interactive way, for the Odysseus Contest 2016-2017.
All this website is designed directly using HTML and CSS by the members of the team.


The members of the The Tiger Team are: Elisabetta, Francesco and Giacomo.



This is the map of the web site:

  • -Home page: main page of the website;
  • -Project: here you can read our work with PowerPoint Online;
  • -Team: we introduct ourselves and how we worked;
  • Add-ons:
    • -References: the websites where we took data and informations;
    • -Follow-up materials: several interesting tools for discovering space exploration.






The ODYSSEUS II Project has received funding from the European Union's Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme. It is funded with nearly two million Euros by the European Commission in the framework of EU HORIZON 2020, started on 1st January 2015 and will run until the 31st December 2017. A strong consortium of fourteen partners and four supporting organizations from eleven European countries will collaborate to provide learning opportunities in space science and technology to European youth. Italian partners are Politecnico di Torino and Altec.
